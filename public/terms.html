1. Educational purpose

The Proto Migration Map is an educational visualization of Proto-Indo-European linguistic and genetic migration patterns. It is designed to help general audiences explore the research presented in Laura Spinney's book Proto: How One Ancient Language Went Global and the underlying academic literature in comparative linguistics, archaeology, and archaeogenetics.

The map is provided free of charge for personal, non-commercial, educational use.

2. Accuracy disclaimer

The territorial extents, migration routes, and dates shown on this map are schematic approximations. They are intended to convey broad historical patterns, not precise historical or geographical boundaries.

  • Territorial circles are simplified representations; actual culture areas were irregularly shaped and contested.
  • Dates are approximate and subject to ongoing scholarly revision.
  • Migration arrows indicate general direction and sequence, not specific routes.
  • Modern political borders are shown for geographic reference only; they have no relationship to ancient territorial divisions.
  • The genetic and linguistic information reflects scholarly consensus as of 2024–2025 and may not incorporate the most recent findings.

We have made every reasonable effort to present accurate information, but we make no representations or warranties about the completeness, reliability, or current accuracy of the content. Do not rely on this map for academic, professional, legal, or any other purpose requiring verified historical accuracy.
