Before you explore

Territorial extents shown on this map are schematic approximations, not historically precise boundaries. Dates are approximate and subject to ongoing scholarly revision. Migration arrows indicate general direction, not specific routes.

Modern political borders are shown for geographic orientation only and have no relationship to ancient territorial divisions.

Data reflects the scholarly consensus of archaeogenetics and comparative linguistics as of 2024–2025. See Terms of Use for full accuracy disclaimer and source attributions.
